Our Story

Ethel's Coffee Shop has been serving the community with passion and dedication since our doors first opened. Named after our beloved founder, Ethel, who believed that great coffee brings people together, we continue her legacy of creating a warm, welcoming space where friends meet, stories are shared, and memories are made.
